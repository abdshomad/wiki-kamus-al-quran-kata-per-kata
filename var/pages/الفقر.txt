!! EXPLANATORY DICTIONARY OF THE HOLY QURAN 

598. AL-FAQR (Poverty) 2:268 (ر ق ف)

Opposite of which is rich 3:181. Poverty is associated with distress 22:28. Charity is to be given to the poor 9:60 who are in need of good things 28:24 but who cannot get or earn them because they are confined in the way of Allah (SWT) 2:273 and it is better to hide and give them charity 2:271 so that they may not f eel insulted. One derivative is used to mean disaster 75:25, poverty itself being a kind of continuous disaster.
