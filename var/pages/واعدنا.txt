!!EXPLANATORY DICTIONARY OF THE HOLY QURAN

211. WAA-ADNAA (We appointed) 2:51 (د ع و)

Other derivatives: Promise 5:9, appointment 20:59, 18:59 and threatening 7:70, 50:45 probably referring to the promised punishment. The devil makes false promises and goes against them 4:120, 14:22. Whereas the promise of Allah (SWT) is true and He does not go against it 46:17, 4:122, 3:9. Allah (SWT) has promised the hypocrites and the infidels hell 9:68 and the believers paradise 9:72
