3. ISM (A Name) 5:4, 61:6 
 
Someone or something can be recognize or identified by his/its name. It is sometimes associated with qualities and / or purpose of a thing like ‘sugar’ for the quality of sweetness and ‘chair’ used for the purpose of sitting, ‘Allah’ representi ng all His Attributes 7:180 etc., (5:4, 61:6)
