99. FIRAASHA (Spreading) 2:22 (ش ر ف)

Other derivatives: The earth is a spreading below opposed to the sky a structure above 2:22, 51:47-48, cattle of low height like sheep, goats etc. suitable for eating as opposed to comparatively tall cattle meant for carrying burden like horses, mules etc. 6:142, moths which normally fly low 101:4, spreading like bed sheets (laid down) 55:54 and raised beds kept on the floor 56:34.
