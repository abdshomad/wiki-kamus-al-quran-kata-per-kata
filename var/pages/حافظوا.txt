!! EXPLANATORY DICTIONARY OF THE HOLY QURAN 
 
535. HAAFIZUU (Be guardians) 2:238 (ظ ف ح)

Other derivatives: Guarding against something 24:31, protecting and taking care of something 12:12, being a custodian or an in charge 12:55,  monitoring,  keeping  an  eye on  to  implement  or  regulate something 5:44, 6:92, 2:255. 

One of the Attributes of Allah (SWT) is ‘Al Hafiiz’ i.e. One Who guards and protects from harmful things, takes care and monitors everything, everywhere and every moment - The Custodian - The Protector - The Monitor.
