171. YAHZANUUN (They grieve)  2:38 (ن ز ح) 

Grief is associated with departure of son 12:13 sorrow 12:84, distress 3:153, weakness 3:139 fear 29:33 et c. The opposite sense is given by coolness or comfort of the eye 20:40. There will be no fear or grief in paradise 7:49.
