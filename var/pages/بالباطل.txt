183. AL BAATIL (Falsehood) 2:42 (ل ط ب)

This word also means something worthless 2:264, vain 47:33 and to falsify 8:8. Falsehood (cannot enter the Quran) 41:42, (that which people call upon besides Allah (SWT) is) falsehood 22:62 (Those who believe in) falsehood (and reject Allah (SWT) are the losers) 29:67. Falsehood (neither originates anything nor reproduces)
34:49. (Allah (SWT) will abolish) falsehood 42:24. (Infidels follow) falsehood 47:3.

The opposite of falsehood is the truth 3:71, 17:81.
