!!EXPLANATORY DICTIONARY OF THE HOLY QURAN
 
24. YUQMUUN (They establish) 2:3 (م و ق)

In many of the verses of the ho ly Quran, derivative of the same root alphabets convey the sense of establishing something: establishing account of deeds 14:41, Torah and Injeel 5:68, agreement 9:7, law 9:36, salat 2:3 etc. The derivatives are used with ‘salat’ meaning establishment of salat (Worship) at numerous pl aces. Any establishment needs an organization, a process, place, timing s of operation etc. We shall Insha Allah refer to these items under the title ‘salat’.
